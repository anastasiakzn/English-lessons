Reading

Marketplace Monday

Katya works for a company that helps small businesses sell their products on online marketplaces. This week, she has been really busy. Three product listings have been removed from the platform without any explanation. New photos have been taken, but they haven't been uploaded yet.

Her colleague Dima was supposed to check everything on Monday. But the work hasn't been done — he forgot. "Has the report been sent to the client yet?" the manager asked. Nobody answered.

By Thursday, most of the problems have been fixed. Katya is tired but happy. She calls her boyfriend: "Let's go to a bar tonight. I need a break!"

Grammar · Theory

Present Perfect Passive

have / has + been + V3
FormStructureExample
+have/has been + V3The product has been listed.
–haven't/hasn't been + V3The order hasn't been sent yet.
?Have/Has + S + been + V3?Has the report been checked?

⚡ Use when the action is done but we focus on the result, not on who did it.
Compare: "I updated the prices." → "The prices have been updated."
